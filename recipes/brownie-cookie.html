Brownie Cookie

Description

A brownie cookie is a cookie specifically made with the ingredients of a brownie, resulting in a fudgy and chocolatey treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.3 ounce) package brownie mix
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) with racks positioned in middle and lower third. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Stir brownie mix, oil, 3 tablespoons of the flour, eggs, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl until just combined, adding up to 1 tablespoon more flour if dough is too loose; fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Drop dough by rounded tablespoons 2 inches apart onto prepared baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are just set, about 10 minutes, rotating pans to opposite racks halfway through. Let cool on baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely and enjoy!
